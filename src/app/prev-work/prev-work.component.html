RDF Weightbridge

Displaying data emitted by a weightbridge

The purpose of this small program is to show real-time weight data of incoming and outgoing garbage trucks and to control the barriers on both sides. Two camera streams are implemented to show the traffic for the client who controls the barriers. On the server side ffmpeg processes the rtsp streams to send it to the client by socket-io. The weightbridge communicates through RS232 with an ATC-1000 RS232/LAN converter. I use the TCP stream emitted by the ATC-1000 to fetch weights and send them to the client by socket-io. Hosted on company VPS. The site is restricted to company employees.
